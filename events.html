Lisa Whitaker
Missionary with Youth With A Mission, Lisa will share about her recent 40-day journey along the ancient Silk Road. The Silk Road is known for its tremendous influence on commerce and communication across Asia, but few realize it was also a means by which philosophies, education and the Gospel were spread. Much of this history has been silenced. Lisa will share some of her team's discoveries of just how influential and present Christianity has been along those ancient trade routes. Come see what God is doing in Central Asia and beyond.

Patti Yoder
Patti Yoder's presentation will center on her 2012 strokes and the near-death experience that birthed Wisdom From Beyond, serving as the catalyst for a profound life shift. By bridging these spiritual insights with the physical and emotional realities of her 14-year recovery, Patti illustrates how to move past the label of "patient" to truly embody the life of a "living miracle." This integration turns lessons from the "other side" into a practical toolkit for modern living, offering a reflective roadmap for finding purpose and shared wisdom through long-term healing.
