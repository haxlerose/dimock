2025 Schedule, 148th Season

All Services begin at 6:30pm usually on Sundays. Saturday service dates are marked in red. Fellowship and refreshments are served following each service. We hope to see you at our services!

The grounds are located a half mile west of Rt. 29 in Dimock, Pa. (See VISIT US for further details.)
There is a great Pavilion on the grounds and a pond. We welcome churches and others to use our facilities. Talk to us about this.
