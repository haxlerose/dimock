2011 Dimock Schedule

In addition to the Sunday evening services, the following events have been scheduled.

June 11, 8 am - Father's Day pancake breakfast in the dining hall. All the women are encouraged to cook breakfast for the guys.

June 25, 7:30 pm - Bingo in the dining hall. Great chance to win prizes.

July - for the whole month the theme will be Christmas in July. Cottage owners are encouraged to decorate their cottages.

July 9, 8 am - Mother's Day pancake breakfast in the dining hall. All the men are encouraged to cook breakfast for the gals.

July 30, 7:30 pm - Christmas party at the pavilion. We will be making ornaments and singing Christmas carols. Also, presents for everyone present. Feel free to bring Christmas cookies. Drinks and a movie will be provided.

August 13, 4 pm - Picnic at the pond. There will be games and a water slide. Hot dogs, hamburgers, and rolls will be provided. Everyone please bring a covered disk. Also bring your own dishes and drinks.
