2014 Dimock Schedule

Theme: Christian Grace/Love One Another

---> All Services begin at 6:00 pm <---


Sunday, July 6 - Saline, an Original Christian Rock and Modern Worship Band. Formed in Central Pennsylvania, the members of Saline bring different musical backgrounds and styles to merge them into a sound that is infectiously original and melodically appealing to the young and the young at heart. They have shared the stage with national recording artists Lincoln Brewster, Apologetix, Paige Armstrong, Everlife and many others. Saline has completed three full length albums of original music, and their first worship album is called My Lord, My Love. It's a can't miss concert and worship experience.

Saturday, July 12 - Open House at Dimock Campground 11:00 am to 3:00 pm Featuring Gospel Band Susquehanna Jamcrackers from Williamsport, Pa., Damian the Magician with live show at 2:00 pm, Cosmo and Connie face painting/balloons; free hot dog bar/ice cream bar. It's a step back in time with old-fashioned games, nature trail.

Sunday, July 13 - Hymn Sing led by Steve Hulslander and his band Susquehanna Jamcrackers.

Sunday, July 20 - Heartsongs Ministries with Karen Knight Karen Knight, formerly portraying Mrs. Noah of Sight and Sound in Lancaster, Pa., brings talent of singing and drama from the musical Noah. Noah, the Musical, has been seen on TBN television and broadcast throughout the world. She has performed in concerts, special events and churches all over the United States and has shared the stage with Ivan Parker, Larry Ford, the Brooklyn Tabernacle Soloists and well-known artist Sandy Pattie. She has ministered to our military troops at the invitation of the Chaplains of the US Army in Southeast Asia and has been a performing artist in Israel. Extended program, 1-1/2 hr.

Sunday, July 27 - Grace Fellowship Church in Tunkhannock.

Sunday, August 3 - Patti Yoder-Clark Ms. Patti Yoder-Clark from New York State will be bringing dramatic portrayals of the brave and powerful women of the Bible using period costumes.

August 10 - The Farm Hands Bluegrass Quartet Bluegrass singers as featured on the Nashville Grand Ole Opry. We welcome them back for a second year.

August 17 - Commandment 11 Scott Jacobs (son of a Jacobs brother) leads this music ministry from Simpsonville, SC, which ministers to prisons and recovery centers. We welcome them back for a third year.

August 24 - Susan Klein & Friends Susan Klein, a nationally-recognized Christian Ventriloquist from Nanticoke, Pa., brings the message through puppetry and song.

August 31 - Closing Communion Service with Rev. Marcia Wilkinson leading. Music by Martha Denkenberger, Steve Hulslander and Linda Schiefer


All Services begin at 6:00 p.m.


Fellowship and refreshments are served in the dining hall following each service.
The grounds are located a half mile west of Rt. 29 in Dimock, Pa.
