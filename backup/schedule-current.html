2023 Dimock Schedule, 146th Season

---> All Services begin at 6:30 pm <---

---> with two of them on Saturdays <---

Sunday, July 2 - Tri State Gospel Singers
They will present a patriotic program

Sunday, July 9 - Wyoming County Chorale under the direction of Doreen Ignatovich
It draws on a vast range of musical genres and time periods for its program material - from Renaissance to Rock, Baroque to Big Band.

Sunday, July 16 - Lisa Whitaker (a member of the campground)
Serving with Youth With A Mission for over 24 years and travelling to 71 countries, she will tell stories of God's faithfulness around the world through stories and pictures.

Sunday, July 23 - Pastor Stephen Pirillo and the NEPA Praise Band
Christmas in July Celebration with a special guest in RED appearing

Saturday, July 29 - The Airport Pickers
They get together every Sunday afternoon at Skyhaven Airport, Tunkhannock, playing old country, bluegrass & gospel

Sunday, August 6 - Layspeaker Brian Wallace
with a special healing service for the community and Dimock Camp Meeting

Sunday, August 13 - Lisa Whittaker

Sunday, August 20 - Pastor Stephen Pirillo and the NEPA Praise Band

Saturday, August 26 - Tunkhannock Airport Pickers

Sunday, September 3 - Communion Service
Led by our own Pastor Steve Baker

Fellowship and refreshments are served in the chapel following each service.
The grounds are located a half mile west of Rt. 29 in Dimock, Pa.
