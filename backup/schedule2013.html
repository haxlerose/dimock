2013 Dimock Schedule

Theme: God's Mission in the Neighborhood - Community Outreach

We will be collecting non-perishables at every concert/program for the local food bank in Montrose.



July 7 - Opening Hymn Sing with Kathy Fisk, Special Ministries Director of Elm Park UMC, Scranton.

July 14 - Gospel Music Ministry of Dan Schall, Zelienople, PA; "And if a lowly singer dries one tear, or soothes one humble human heart in pain, be sure His homely verse to God is dear, and not one stanza has been sung in vain."

July 21 - We welcome back the Jacobs Brothers - Mike and Bob Jacobs, and Mike Lawver - of Dillsburg, PA, who have been sharing the gospel of Jesus Christ through song and testimony since 1962 across the US.

July 28 - Rev. David Erickson and Saving Grace Singers; he is the minister of the United Baptist Church-Taylor, and will share inspiring words about the food pantry ministry of the church.

August 5 - We welcome back the Daryl Willow Ministries, "a gospel music, preaching and testimony ministry" from Swengel, PA.

August 11 - The Farmhands Bluegrass Quartet from Nashville, a bluegrass gospel group featuring two longtime members of the Grand Ole Opry.

August 18 - Commandment 11, Scott Jacobs (son of a Jacobs brother) leads this music ministry from Simpsonville, SC, which ministers to prisons and recovery centers.

August 25 - Sherri Danae and Cheryl of Precious Seed, a mother-daughter duo from Dubois.

September 1 - Seasonal concluding service of Holy Communion with the Rev. Marsha Wilkinson of the Campground and Elverson, PA.


All Services begin at 7:00 p.m.
Fellowship and refreshments are served in the dining hall following each service.
The grounds are located a half mile west of Rt. 29 in Dimock, Pa.
