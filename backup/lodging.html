Lodging at Dimock

Lewis Lodge, a cottage named in honor of the Rev. Joshua Lewis, who was an integral part of the Dimock grounds in its earliest years, is available. It has three bedrooms, a bath and kitchen facilities. A donation of $25 per night or $125 per week will help the Association maintain its facilities.
