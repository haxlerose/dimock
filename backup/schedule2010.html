2010 Dimock Schedule

July 4 - Opening Hymn Sing
Rev. Barbara Bouton-Snyder of Waverly United Methodist Church and accompanist Martha Denkenberger of Montrose United Methodist Church.

July 11 (Founders Day) - 200 Years of Susquehanna County Methodism
Robert Baker, Co-Archivist, United Methodist Church's Susquehanna Conference.

July 18 - Pastor Bob Kadlecik of Bridgewater Baptist Church offering a "Short Challenge" Concert with Jeff Hughes & Relevant who bring their message about the Word of Christ through music.

July 25 - Brothers in Grace, based in souch-central Pennsylvania, has ministered throughout the Mid-Atlantic region, delighting audiences with their unique Southern gospel style music and tight harmony.

August 1 - Pastor Richard Spering & the Retta Singers who have joined traditional folk and gospel music in praise of Jesus Christ.
Also, congregational participation.

August 8 - George Wesley and his one man band will give an informal talk about being a descendent of Charles Wesley.

August 15 - Barb Locey and the Dimock Church Youth

August 22 - Dunnemans of Binghamton, N.Y., are a traveling musical ministry who blend their talents and voices in a wonderful variety of music that transcends denominational lines and lifts up the name of Jesus.

August 29 - Dee Butler, Pastoral Associate Retired (Holy Name of Mary RC Church, Montrose) will present musical selections with family and friends.

Sept 5 - Rev. Bill Brown concludes the season with the Service of Holy Communion.

All Services begin at 7:00 p.m.
Refreshments are served in the dining hall following each service.
The grounds are located a half mile west of Rt. 29 in Dimock, Pa.
