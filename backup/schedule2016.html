2016 Dimock Schedule

---> All Services begin at 6:30 pm <---


Sunday, July 3 - Hymn Sing
Season opens with Hymn Sing led by Jean Grube and Martha Denkenberger. Come and join in all the old favorites.

Sunday, July 10 - Farm Hands Quartet
Enjoy an evening of bluegrass music with favorite musicians, including veterans of the Grand Ole Opry.

Sunday, July 17 - The Vocal Accord Barbershop Quartet
Songs for All Occasions sung by a new group to the Dimock Campground. Performances are sprinkled with comedy, serious, novelty and a few jokes.

Sunday, July 24 - Williamson Branch
Bring the kids! This family of five will delight all ages with their bluegrass harmony and musical gifts.

Sunday, July 31 - Worship and Music
The Rev. Ron Wenzinger, pastor of Sarah Jane Johnson Memorial United Methodist Church , Johnson City, NJ, will preach and his music team will lead music.

Sunday, August 7 - One Voice Trio and Sunday School Choirs
Contemporary worship provided by the Friends of Dimock Community Christian Church and the One Voice Trio.

August 14 - Worship with The Reverend Marian Hartman
District Superintendent, Wilkes-Barre District of the Susquehanna Conference

August 21 - Step by Step Concert and Worship
Steve Perillo of Making a Difference Ministries/Unity in Music will lead worship to bring people together through faith based non-denominational music.

August 28 - Mission District of the Evangelical Lutheran Church
The Mission District of the Evangelical Lutheran Church of America, NE PA, will lead music and worship as well as a short explanation of their mission activities.

September 4 - Season Ending Worship and Communion
The Rev. Canon Charles Cesaratti will lead the final summer program with worship and Holy Communion. Father Charles (as he is known to many around Susquehanna County) served St. Paul's in Montrose as Interim Rector from 2006 to 2009.


All Services begin at 6:30 p.m.


Fellowship and refreshments are served in the dining hall following each service.
The grounds are located a half mile west of Rt. 29 in Dimock, Pa.
