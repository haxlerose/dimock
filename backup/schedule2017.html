2017 Dimock Schedule

---> All Services begin at 6:30 pm <---


Sunday, July 2 - Hymn Sing
We will open the summer with an old fashioned hymn sing. Bring your requests with you.

Sunday, July 9 - The Reminders
The Reminders bring a lifetime of music to the stage and the studio. They love doing this, you can tell. Their sound is a unique blend of Americana and Folk Rock with deep roots in blues, country and gospel. Audiences across the country have agreed, with overwhelming enthusiasm, that they need to do it more often. They just have that wonderful chemistry that is so hard to define, but so much fun to see and hear. Individually, they've been honored by the Recording Industry Association of America (The Grammy people), the National Endowment for the Arts, Billboard Magazine, ASCAP, BMI, and many others. You don't wanna miss them live. They've done enough in their careers to get you there, they'll do enough live to get you comin' back.

Sunday, July 16 - Good News
Good News is a mixed trio whose members include Al & Laurie Magill singing lead and tenor, with Russ Sears as baritone. Traveling through Pennsylvania, New York and beyond, the group's mission is to spread the gospel message of Christ's redeeming love and salvation, and to encourage the church in their daily walk with the Lord as they look forward to His return. Their musical lineup presents a variety of styles including the old gospel standards and hymns, their original compositions and acapella, as well as some new powerful renditions, presented in a way that will lead the audience in a meaningful worship experience.

Sunday, July 23 - Sue & Kelly Pratt with the Re-entry Choir, and speaker Mike Hawthorne
The Pratt's are a music ministry team that has been performing at churches, festivals, restaurants, parks and picnics for over 13 year. Nine years ago God called them to join the Exodus Prison & Aftercare Ministries (EPAM) team bringing their musical gifts to the women's worship/praise services on Sunday afternoons at the Susquehanna City Correctional Facility. The women's voices of praise and music have blossomed into the Re-Entry Choir. Mike Hawthorne, also an EPAM member, will be sharing his testimony.

Sunday, July 30 - The "Fisher Clan" (Robert, Rae Belle, Sally and perhaps others)
We are looking for fellow Christians to come join in a relaxed, enjoyable evening of music and fun. Trusting the Lord for a joyful time with music, His word, and a good time together!! If you have a favorite or something to share, please let us know. We love the Lord, music, and fun being together!!We look forward to what God has for each of us at this gathering. Reaching out to encourage us all in our walk with God.

Sunday, August 6 - Kim Kielel
The Lord has given Kim a heart to sing, and her greatest joy is leading people to the foot of His throne through music. She had had the privilege of sharing her gift of song in concerts, conferences, camp meetings and the local church for more than 18 years. Kim and her husband Roy live in Johnson City, New York, with their three children.

August 13 - The Tri-State Gospel Choir
Founded in 2014, the group has performed in many parades, fairs, festivals and held concerts. They bring a new and innovative musical presentation that will touch lives of people for our Lord and Savior. They perform a wide variety of gospel/Christian music styles as well as Patriotic.

August 20 - Jean Starzec & Corky Staats
bring their music gifts as a team. Jean loves praising God through music. Corky writes and shares original songs of the spiritual journey.

August 27 - Communion Service
Led by Rev. Jane Pykus from the South Montrose Community Church & New Milford United Methodist Church and music by Saving Graces, which is a women's group from South Montrose Community Church. They have been singing for close to 15 year. They use their music to share the love of God through classic and contemporary Christian songs. Saving Graces has traveled to New York, New Jersey, and throughout Pennsylvania spreading God's word. PA, will lead music and worship as well as a short explanation of their mission activities.

September 3 - Invitation to the Community Table
led by Mac Huslander & Squeek Hawley.


All Services begin at 6:30 p.m.


Fellowship and refreshments are served in the chapel following each service.
The grounds are located a half mile west of Rt. 29 in Dimock, Pa.
